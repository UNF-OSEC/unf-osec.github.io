Osprey Security is a club with the main focus on Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking. We provide a controlled environment where members can learn, experience, and practice varying degrees of Cyber Security topics daily. Over the course of a semester members have the opportunity to attend workshops or presentations from IT professionals, business leaders, and University professors. In addition, we facilitate a committed group of inexperienced and experienced members to participate in a wide variety of both local and national Cyber Security competitions.

We are always open to new members, and to learn new things, if you have suggestions, or want to give a presentation reach out at the about section.
